For Emma, Forever Ago

For Emma, Forever Ago was the first project assignment I had in my Image class. It forced me to try my hand in photography as well as advanced editing with Photoshop, which were both previously out of my comfort zone. This project is based off of the song For Emma by Bon Iver. This song has always resonated with me and each image captures the feelings evoked in the lyrics. Here is the full project.
